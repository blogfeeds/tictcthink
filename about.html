About Tic Tac Think

Welcome to Tic Tac Think! This game is a modern twist on the classic Tic Tac Toe, designed to challenge your strategic thinking and provide an engaging experience for players of all skill levels.

Game Concept

In Tic Tac Think, you will compete against the computer in an exciting battle of wits. Each player takes turns placing their symbol—either "X" or "O"—on a 3x3 game board. The objective is to align three of your symbols in a row, column, or diagonal before your opponent does.

Key Features

  • Challenge Against AI: Test your skills against an AI designed to provide varying levels of challenge. Can you outsmart the computer?
  • Multiple Difficulty Levels: Choose from different difficulty levels to tailor your gaming experience, from beginner to expert, ensuring there’s a challenge for everyone.
  • Engaging Design: Enjoy a clean and attractive interface that makes each game a delightful experience.
  • Game Statistics: Track your progress with game statistics displaying wins, losses, and draws.

How to Play

  1. Choose your symbol (X or O).
  2. Take turns placing your symbol on the board.
  3. Aim to align three of your symbols while blocking the computer from winning.
  4. Enjoy each match and enhance your strategy!
